requirements to become a tutor

  1. Teaching experience or experience working with Children.
  2. Access to a good laptop in good working condition.
  3. Access to a stable internet connection.
  4. Excellent communication skills.
  5. Proficient in online products like Zoom, Google Classroom and so on.
  6. Willingness to learn on the job.
  7. Must be willing to work at least 7 hours weekly.
