About Us

Who Are We?

The Light Tutors is a tutoring academy founded during the pandemic in a bid to make education accessible despite the challenges of social distancing and timing. We work towards bridging the gap in education, and are constantly looking for ways to make learning as easy and seamless as possible. TLT offers personalised tutoring sessions in Mathematics, English, and Sciences to Students in KS2 up to GCSE level, elementary (Grades K–5), middle (Grades 6–8) and high (Grades 9–12). We also offer bespoke content for languages such as Yoruba and French for beginners and intermediate Students.

Our Mission

  • To celebrate all achievements.
  • To give Students a good foundation to build on.
  • To give Students the tools to enjoy their learning.
  • To provide an inclusive learning environment where children with special needs can thrive in education
  • To become leaders in impactful learning programmes, not only for academic prowess but for character building.
  • To adopt individualised learning approaches that allows Students to learn to work more efficiently even through different types of fun activities

About

The Light Tutors is a tutoring company formed in 2020 with a vision of giving Students good academic foundations to build on and to help bridge gaps in education.
